Bachelor of Business Administration

Developing future business leaders

Course Overview

The Bachelor of Business Administration program prepares students for leadership roles in the corporate world. Through a comprehensive curriculum covering management, finance, marketing, and operations, students develop the skills needed for successful business careers.

Key Features:

  • 3-year undergraduate program
  • Industry partnerships
  • Case study approach
  • Business incubation center
  • Corporate internships
  • Leadership development
