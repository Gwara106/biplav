Bachelor of Science in Chemistry

Discover the molecular world and its applications

Course Overview

The Bachelor of Science in Chemistry program offers comprehensive training in organic, inorganic, physical, and analytical chemistry. Students gain hands-on experience through extensive laboratory work and research projects.

Key Features:

  • 3-year undergraduate program
  • Well-equipped chemistry laboratories
  • Industrial training opportunities
  • Research projects
  • Industry collaboration
  • Environmental chemistry focus
