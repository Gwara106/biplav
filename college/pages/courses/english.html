Bachelor of Arts in English Literature

Explore the world through the lens of literature

Course Overview

The Bachelor of Arts in English Literature program offers a comprehensive study of literary works across different periods, cultures, and genres. Students develop critical thinking, analytical skills, and creative expression through the study of classic and contemporary literature.

Key Features:

  • 3-year undergraduate program
  • Extensive library resources
  • Creative writing workshops
  • Literary seminars and discussions
  • Publication opportunities
  • Drama and theater activities
