Discover the beauty and power of mathematical thinking

Course Overview

The Bachelor of Science in Mathematics program offers a comprehensive study of pure and applied mathematics. Students develop strong analytical and problem-solving skills while exploring advanced mathematical concepts.

Key Features:

  • 3-year undergraduate program
  • Advanced mathematics laboratory
  • Research opportunities
  • Expert faculty members
  • Regular seminars and workshops
  • Career guidance in various fields
